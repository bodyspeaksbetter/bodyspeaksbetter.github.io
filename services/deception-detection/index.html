Lie Detection

In an ideal society, there will not be any space for lies. But we live in a world of deception; you want to play or not, you are in the game. The question is if you want to win. How many times have you heard people saying,

“I didn’t mean a single word of what I said”?

Sometimes, people lie to protect someone from being hurt or lie for a good cause; but it doesn’t happen often. So, how would you know, if someone is lying to you. Here is where we come as a buddy to you; to make you understand the technique of Lie detection. In certain contexts this topic is also known as deception detection.

Call us for a session and you will know everything about a lie and the liar; the categories of liars, types of lies, the behaviour and psychology behind every liar, the popular myths about lies/liars etc.
